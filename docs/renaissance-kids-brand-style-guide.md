# Renaissance Kids Brand Style Guide

## Mission Statement

“Light up learning through the arts.“™

## Brand Identity

Renaissance Kids is warm, creative, community-focused, professional yet approachable, and structured yet playful.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

- Teal: `#2F6B65` (backgrounds, headers)
- Peach: `#F7C4A5` (accents, highlights)
- Navy: `#1C1C1C` (text, borders)

### Accent Colors

- Brand Red: `#E92929`
- Brand Blue: `#2B59C3`
- Brand Yellow: `#FFE600`
- Brand Green: `#48B749`
- Brand Purple: `#8028A0`
- Brand Orange: `#F05A22`

## Typography

- Headings (Primary): `Comic Sans MS, Chalkboard, cursive`
- Headings (Secondary): `Century Gothic, Arial, sans-serif`
- Body: `Century Gothic, Arial, sans-serif` at 16px base
- Code/Data: `Monaco, Courier, monospace`
